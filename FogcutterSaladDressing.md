## Fogcutter Salad Dressing

- 2 c canola oil 
- ¾ c water 

- ¾ c red wine vinegar 

- 2.25 oz Dijon mustard 
- 3.125 oz Worcestershire 
- 1/16 oz Tabasco 
- 1 T salt 
- 1 ½ tsp sugar 

**Mix all ingredients together with whisk. Pour into jar or other container and refrigerate. Keeps indefinitely.**
